Common questions

Is the first class really free?
Yes — for all capoeira classes (Kids, Youth, and Teens & Adults), your first class is free for new students. Just show up. No payment, no card required up front.
What should I bring?
Comfortable clothes you can move in, a water bottle, and shoes with a smooth sole (dance shoes or flat sneakers) so you can turn easily without marking the floor. For capoeira, a t-shirt and comfortable workout pants. No prior experience ever needed.
How much does it cost?
$30/class for capoeira and salsa. $25/class for yoga, mobility, and fitness. Packages are available — ask Claudia at the studio or mention it in the notes below.
Do I need a partner for salsa?
No partner needed. Our Absolute Beginner class teaches you to dance solo or with a partner — we rotate so everyone gets practice with different leads & follows.
Can I just walk in?
Absolutely. Walk-ins are always welcome. Booking ahead just helps us know to expect you and saves you a spot if a class is busy.
Do you offer private lessons?
Yes — capoeira with Uriel and salsa with Luis. Pick "Private Lesson" in the form and we'll reach out to schedule.
Where are you located?
2029 Mt. Diablo Blvd, Walnut Creek, CA 94596. Free street parking and a public lot nearby.
